Abilities

Incongruent Outcome: Abilities performed near Liora produce skewed results; attacks veer off-course or spells activate with altered vectors. Results are distorted, never nullified.(10 meter radius)
Causality Lag: Events occur out of order. Actions resolve before their causes, or consequences arrive late, creating brief combat disorientation.
False Constants: Physics (gravity, light, sound) becomes unreliable near her, gaining inconsistent biases and unpredictable shifts in weight or echo.(5 meters radius around her)
Chaos Anchor: Liora lacks absolute control. Effects apply to her and allies indiscriminately and weaken in structured environments.

Personality

Playful, irreverent, and unsettlingly calm in dangerous situations. Liora treats conflict like a game and authority like a suggestion. Despite her joking demeanor, she occasionally displays moments of sharp insight and unsettling seriousness, hinting that she understands far more than she lets on.

Backstory

Liora wasn't born from a family, but from a mathematical error. She first appeared in the Great Library of Aethelgard, not as a child, but as a smudge on a blueprint that eventually grew a heartbeat. No one knows which "relative" she belongs to because her genealogy keeps rewriting itself in the archives. For years, she was a curiosity studied by mages, but order couldn't hold her. One afternoon, while a professor was explaining the laws of gravity, Liora decided she liked the ceiling better, and the entire lecture hall "flipped" to accommodate her. She walked out the window and hasn't looked back. Now, she wanders as a walking glitch in the world’s source code—neither hero nor villain, just the pebble in the shoe of destiny. She claims she is looking for the "Librarian" who first tried to categorize her, simply to see if his head will explode if she tells him a joke with no punchline.
